### Purpose
This document outlines all graphics, overlays, text elements, and motion graphics needed throughout the video to enhance communication and visual appeal.

## Graphics Style Guide

### Brand Guidelines
- **Primary Colors:** 
  - GitHub Black: #24292e
  - GitHub Blue: #0366d6
  - Accent Gold: #FFD700 (for emphasis)
  - White: #FFFFFF
- **Typography:**
  - Headers: Bold, sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat Bold)
  - Body: Medium weight, sans-serif (e.g., Open Sans)
  - Code: Monospace (e.g., Fira Code, Consolas)
- **Style:** Modern, clean, tech-forward
- **Animation:** Smooth, professional (0.3-0.5s transitions)

## Technical Specifications

### Video Overlay Settings
- **Resolution:** Match video (1920x1080 or 4K)
- **Format:** PNG with alpha channel (transparency)
- **Color Space:** sRGB
- **Bit Depth:** 32-bit (8-bit per channel + alpha)

### Animation Timing
- **Fast transitions:** 0.2-0.3 seconds
- **Standard transitions:** 0.4-0.6 seconds
- **Slow reveals:** 0.8-1.2 seconds
- **Ease:** Ease-in-out for smoothness

### Safe Areas
- **Title Safe:** 10% from edges
- **Action Safe:** 5% from edges
- **Text Minimum Size:** 36pt for readability

### Export Settings
- **File Format:** MOV with PNG codec or PNG sequence
- **Alpha Channel:** Yes (preserve transparency)
- **Frame Rate:** Match video (24, 30, or 60 fps)

## Notes for Graphics Team
- Maintain consistent brand identity throughout
- Keep graphics subtle - they support, don't dominate
- Ensure all text is legible at video resolution
- Test on multiple screen sizes
- Consider colorblind accessibility
- Use animation to guide viewer attention
- Sync graphic reveals with audio/dialogue
- Export at highest quality for flexibility in edit
